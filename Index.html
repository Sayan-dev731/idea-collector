Campus Ideas Hub

Collect, organize, and summarize student ideas on sustainability and innovation — powered by Microsoft.

About the initiative

Colleges often struggle to organize and summarize large volumes of student ideas. This app streamlines collection (via Microsoft Forms), centralizes storage (SharePoint/Excel), and creates judge-friendly summaries — all inside the Microsoft ecosystem.

  • Microsoft Forms: Structured student submissions with categories.
  • SharePoint/Excel: Secure storage with metadata.
  • Power BI: Visual dashboards for decision-making.
  • Copilot: Quick summaries for judges and faculty.
Tip: Sign in with your Microsoft account to sync data with SharePoint/Excel via Graph API. Otherwise, data saves locally in your browser for demo.
